# Zomato Safety AI  
### Earn without worry — your income stays protected

--------------
# The Problem

Zomato delivery partners lose out on income when the things go sideways. Heavy rain, blistering heat, pollution, sudden curfews — none of this is in their control, but their earnings depend on it... Sometimes their pay drops by 20–30% in a single day, which hits hard because many rely on daily wages.

Right now, there is no simple system that supports them in this situation.

-------------------

## Real Life Example

Here’s what actually happens.

Ravi works as a Zomato delivery partner. On a normal day, he pulls in about ₹800.

But let’s say there’s a serious downpour:
- He only manages to make about ₹200  
- That’s ₹600 gone in just one day

This isn’t rare. It happens pretty often, and over time, it adds up to a big problem.

---

## What We’re Building

We’re setting up something simple called **Zomato Safety AI**.

The idea’s basic: if you can’t deliver because of bad weather or other external issues, you get compensated — automatically.

No forms, no nagging support, no waiting. The system checks conditions and pays out on its own.

---

## How It Works

1. You register in the app.
2. Pick a weekly protection plan.
3. The system keeps an eye out for:
   - Rain
   - Heat
   - Air pollution
   - Local restrictions

4. If things get rough:
   - Claim gets triggered instantly
   - Money lands in your account

No paperwork. No delay.

---

## Weekly Plan — The Simple Breakdown

We’re keeping the plan plain and clear.

- Weekly payment: About ₹50
- Coverage: Up to ₹500 each week

For example:
- Heavy rain? You get ₹100
- Crazy heat? That’s ₹80
- High pollution? Add ₹80
- Curfew/restriction? ₹150

Amounts may shift a bit based on your area’s risk levels.

---

## How Claims Are Triggered

Our system takes a parametric approach.

We collect data, check for bad conditions, and if the threshold’s crossed — payout happens. That’s it.

Triggers include:
- Heavy rainfall
- High temperatures
- High AQI (air quality index)
- Restricted zones

It’s quick, clear, and avoids messy delays.

---

## Where the AI Comes In

We’re not getting fancy.

AI helps with:
- Spotting risky areas
- Adjusting your weekly premium if needed
- Catching fake claims (wrong GPS, repeated requests)

This keeps everything efficient and fair for everyone.
